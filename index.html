Valorant

Valorant is a tactical first-person shooter developed by Riot Games, combining precise gunplay with unique agent abilities.

Agent Information

  • Jett's agile and evasive fighting style lets her take risks no one else can

  • Viper deploys an array of poisonous chemical devices to control the field

  • Forged in the heart of Mexico, Reyna dominates single combat.

  • Killjoy secures the battlefield with ease using her arsenal of inventions.

"As much as this world hurt me, it's mine, and I will kill to defend it."

Playing Valorant offers a thrilling mix of tactical gameplay, strategic teamwork, and fast-paced action.
